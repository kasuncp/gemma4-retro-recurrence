Figure 4 · Plan 5 · Hero

The inversion.

Switching from 8-shot Wei et al. exemplars to a zero-shot prompt under the IT model's chat template adds +41.6 percentage points on the same 500 problems, using less compute per problem. The 30% plateau was a prompt-format artifact, not a reasoning ceiling.
